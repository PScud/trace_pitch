The Problem

Riders spend thousands on suspension and tune it by feel.

A serious mountain bike costs $5,000–$15,000. The fork and shock alone run $800–$2,500. Fox, RockShox, Öhlins — riders obsess over their components.

Then they go ride, something feels off, they make a guess, and try again. The suspension is moving 500 times per second over every rock and root. None of that data is being captured.

Pay a suspension tuner $150 for a session? They work by experience — educated feel, not data. The gap between what riders spend and what they know about their setup is enormous.

The Solution

TRACE — sensor to insight in one workflow.

TRACE is two things: a small wireless sensor that clips onto the suspension, and an iPhone app that turns the data into specific tuning recommendations.

1

Clip on the sensor

Small wireless unit mounts to fork and shock. Linear pot reads position 500 times per second. Transmits over Bluetooth. No wires, no complicated setup.

2

Open the app, calibrate, ride

Select your bike and setup. One-tap calibration with the bike off the ground. Hit record and put the phone in your pocket. App runs in the background, lock screen shows stop/pause.

3

Review your data

Fork tab and Shock tab show full position analysis — stroke counts, compression and rebound speed histograms, travel zone usage, sag measurement. Everything your suspension did on that run.

4

Check the balance

Balance tab shows fork vs rear working together. Are they moving proportionally? Is one dominating? Speed balance table breaks it down across low speed, high speed, overall.

5

Get your recommendations

Insights tab delivers specific, actionable suggestions — not "your rebound might be slow" but "add 3 clicks low speed rebound on your fork (currently 6 from closed)." Referenced to your exact setup.

Why TRACE

Not just data. Answers.

🎯

Specific suggestions

Not "your rebound seems slow." Exactly which adjuster, how many clicks, and why — referenced to your actual setup values.

🚲

Knows your bike

Head tube angle, leverage curve, travel figures. The app understands the mechanical system and calculates real vertical wheel movement, not just raw sensor travel.

🧠

Learns your riding

Recommendations improve over sessions as the AI learns how you ride and what adjustments you actually respond to.

⚡

Consumer hardware

Clip on, pair, ride. No wires, no technical setup, no race-team expertise required. Designed to be on and off in 60 seconds at the trailhead.
